# Color Palette Notes
All colors are defined in okhsl.
There are two semantic variants: cyan (hue: 200) and purple (hue: 270). You can choose either one.

You can preview okhsl colors at [ok-color-picker](https://ok-color-picker.netlify.app/).
| Color | Purpose | Context | Opacity Guideline |
|-------|---------|---------|-------------------|
| `accentAlpha` | Active states | Selection background | Default: 20, Scrollbar: 25/35, Minimap: 10/20 |
| `baseAlpha` | Hover/Focus states | Hover/focus background | Default: 15, Minimap: 25 |
| `accent` | Primary accent | Highlights, button | Not hovering: 90, hovering: 100 |
| `border` | Border | Dividers, subtle outlines | — |
| `borderStrong` | Prominent border | Focus borders (input, titlebar and statusbar border) | — |
| `textFocus` | High emphasis text |  Text on selection, editor text | — |
| `textNormal` | Standard text | Body text, regular content | — |
| `textSub` | Low emphasis text | Hints, sub info, disabled text | — |
| `bgLayer1-6` | Background hierarchy | Containers, panels | — |
| `buttonSecondary` | Secondary button | Secondary button foreground | — |
| Primary colors | Semantic colors | Red, Yellow, Green, LightBlue, Blue, Purple, Black | — |
